# Supply Chain Agent (Domain)

## Responsibility
Maps and analyzes global supply chain dependencies, logistics bottlenecks, and supplier risks.

## Inputs
- Bill of materials (BOM) data
- Logistics reports
- Geopolitical news

## Outputs
- Supply chain graph nodes
- Risk assessment scores (High/Medium/Low)
- Disruption impact analysis

## Constraints
- Must account for multi-tier dependencies
- Privacy aware (obfuscate sensitive supplier names if required)
